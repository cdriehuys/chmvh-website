Services

Chapel Hill Mobile Veterinary Housecalls provides comprehensive care for dogs and cats in their home environment. Dr. Driehuys is equipped for complete physical exams, vaccinations, health certificates, laboratory diagnostics, and treatment of outpatient conditions. This service is convenient for clients and ideal for many patients. Most pets are more comfortable and less anxious at home than in a veterinary hospital.

Services Offered

Puppy and kitten check-ups

Adult wellness exams

Senior pet evaluations

Vaccinations

Individual schedules based on each patient's health.

Intestinal parasite tests

Heartworm tests, treatment, and prevention

Deworming

Nail trims, clipping of matted fur

Flea and tick treatment and prevention

Diagnostic laboratory tests

Bloodwork, urinalysis, and clinical pathology by a commercial veterinary laboratory

Behavior consultations and modification programs

Diagnosis and treatment for a variety of ailments

Including, but not limited to: skin disease, ear infections, allergies, vomiting, diarrhea, urinary tract disease, arthritis, limping, eye disorders, diabetes, Cushing's disease, and wounds

Nutrition evaluation and diet recommendations

Oral and dental preventive care

Microchip services

Blood pressure readings

Digital Tonometry

Eye pressure measurement for glaucoma

Palliative care and euthanasia for established patients

Hospitalization

For patients who require inpatient hospitalization, surgery, or radiology (x-rays or ultrasound), CHMVH has referral arrangements with local primary care and referral veterinary hospitals.

Payment Options

We accept many forms of payment, including cash, check, and credit card. We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and debit cards. Payment is expected at the time that services are rendered.
